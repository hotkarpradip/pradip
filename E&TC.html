Electronics & Telecommunication Engineering

Department

What is E&TC Engineering?

Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering is a branch of engineering that deals with electronic devices, circuits, communication systems, and signal processing.

What E&TC Engineers Do

  • Design electronic circuits and systems
  • Develop communication technologies
  • Work with wireless and network systems
  • Maintain and test electronic equipment

Major Fields

  • Communication Engineering
  • Signal Processing
  • Embedded Systems
  • VLSI Design
  • Wireless Technology

Importance

E&TC engineering is essential for modern communication systems like mobile networks, internet, satellites, and broadcasting.

Common Projects

  • Mobile communication systems
  • IoT devices
  • Satellite communication
  • Embedded systems

Skills Required

  • Electronics knowledge
  • Programming skills
  • Problem-solving
  • Analytical thinking

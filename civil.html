Civil Engineering

Department

What is Civil Engineering?

Civil engineering is the branch of engineering that deals with the design, construction, and maintenance of infrastructure such as buildings, roads, bridges, and dams.

What Civil Engineers Do

  • Plan and design construction projects
  • Analyze materials and soil
  • Supervise construction work
  • Ensure safety of structures

Major Fields

  • Structural Engineering
  • Geotechnical Engineering
  • Transportation Engineering
  • Environmental Engineering
  • Water Resources Engineering

Importance

Civil engineering helps in building infrastructure, improving transportation, and supporting economic development.

Common Projects

  • Roads and highways
  • Bridges and flyovers
  • Buildings
  • Dams and water systems

Skills Required

  • Problem-solving
  • Mathematics
  • Design skills
  • Project management

Computer Engineering Department

This course of study for a Diploma in Computer Engineering is to complete in Three years as regular course.
Computer Engineering is an interdisciplinary field, both in its origin and application. Computer Engineering plays an important role in virtually all fields, including science, medicine, music, art, business, law and communication.
Computer has revolutionized not only the data processing and storage, but also the quality of life,
communication, manufacturing and transportation. PC, Laptop, Internet, and Teleconference have become a household commodity.
The department works towards empowering students for professional and personal excellence. It has created a vibrant knowledge-based environment which motivates students to explore new ideas and perform better in academics.
Whether your goal is to become a practicing computer engineer or take a few courses to develop a basic understanding of computer skills for application in other fields,
the department of computer science at SVSMD’s polytechnic is committed to helping you again the computer knowledge you need.
With the best infrastructure available, the department motivates its students to think of innovative and unending possibilities in the field of Computer Science,
and transform these ideas into technological requirements of the industry. The Department boasts of lab facilities that any student of this field might require.
Career Scope :
On completion of Diploma in computer engineering, plenty of career Opportunities exist in (a) IT industry.
(b) Telecommunication Industry.
(c) E-Commerce Services.
(d) Financial services sector.
(e) Graphic design & art.
(f) Biotechnology Industry.
(g) Nano-technology etc.
Career options : o After completion of diploma computer engineering the students can go for higher studies like BE/ B.Tech course to Direct Second Year. o They can find openings as system developers and programmers. o They get sufficient opportunity to learn PC hardware to become hardware Engineers and network Engineers. o They have infinite opportunities in the field of web and multimedia designing. Most MNC’s prefer diploma students for BPO and call center jobs.

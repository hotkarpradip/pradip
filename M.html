What is Mechanical Engineering?

Mechanical engineering is a branch of engineering that deals with the design, manufacturing, and maintenance of machines, tools, and mechanical systems.

What Mechanical Engineers Do

  • Design machines and mechanical systems
  • Develop engines and manufacturing equipment
  • Analyze mechanical problems
  • Improve efficiency of systems

Major Fields

  • Thermal Engineering
  • Manufacturing Engineering
  • Automobile Engineering
  • Robotics
  • Mechatronics

Importance

Mechanical engineering is essential for developing machines, vehicles, and industrial systems that make life easier and support modern industries.

Common Projects

  • Engines and turbines
  • Robotic systems
  • Automobiles
  • Manufacturing machines

Skills Required

  • Problem-solving
  • Mathematics and physics
  • Design and creativity
  • Technical knowledge
